Description

The Journal of the Martin Institute | International Studies has been published by the Martin Institute at University of Idaho since 2009 as a showcase of noteworthy student research.

Volumes feature policy papers, white papers, and research articles written by students in the Martin Academy, Martin Scholars programs, the International Studies capstone course, and others from across campus. Submissions are selected and evaluated by a team of student editors. The Martin Institute Program in International Studies publishes both a print and online version of the journal.
